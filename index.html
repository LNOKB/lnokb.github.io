I am a graduate student in the Department of Psychology at The University of Tokyo.

My main research area is in Human Vision and Cognitive Science. My area of expertise is scene and color perception. As a vision scientist, one of my ultimate aspirations is to understand how humans perceive the world in the peripheral visual field.

If you want to work with me, please send email to lanaokubo[at]gmail.com.
